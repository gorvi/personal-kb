## 二、话术模板

### 询盘回复模板

#### 初次询盘
```
Hi [Name],

Thanks for your inquiry about [Product].

Here are the details you requested:
- [Specification]
- [Price]
- [MOQ]
- [Lead time]

Could you share more about your:
1. Target market
2. Expected quantity
3. Application scenario

This will help me provide a more accurate quote.

Best,
[Your name]
```

#### 跟进模板
```
Hi [Name],

Just following up on my last email about [Product].

I understand you're busy. To save your time, here are the most popular options our clients choose:

[Option A] - [Price] - Best for [Scenario]
[Option B] - [Price] - Best for [Scenario]

Would you like to schedule a quick call to discuss?

Best,
[Your name]
```
